Who I Am

My name is Purushothaman, i am from India, born in Chennai. I am a Post graduate in MCA Master of Computer Applications with 4 years of experience in E-Publishing Industry.

I'm an eBooks Developer and Video Editor with an eye for details and a passion for perfection.

In high school, I was that kid that seemed to be average student paying attention in studies…

When I started college I originally had the idea of going into animation. But due to my economic problem I was suppose to end up my dream. I was hooked. The internet was my outlet. A new way to show and share my passions.

I do the work I do because I love it. I like to talk less. I like to make the web a better place. Hopefully we cross paths in the real world someday and we can make it a better place together.
